Our Services

Join us for worship, fellowship, and spiritual growth

Sunday Worship Service

Sundays at 10:00 AM

Join us for our main worship service featuring inspiring music, meaningful messages, and a welcoming community atmosphere. All are welcome to participate in this uplifting experience.

  • Contemporary worship music
  • Biblical teaching
  • Children's ministry available
  • Fellowship time after service

Bible Study

Wednesdays at 7:00 PM

Dive deeper into God's word with our weekly Bible study sessions. We explore scripture together, discuss its relevance to our daily lives, and grow in understanding and faith.

  • Interactive discussions
  • Study materials provided
  • Small group format
  • All skill levels welcome

Youth Fellowship

Fridays at 6:00 PM

A dynamic program designed for teenagers and young adults. We combine fun activities with spiritual growth, creating lasting friendships and strong faith foundations.

  • Ages 13-25 welcome
  • Games and activities
  • Mentorship opportunities
  • Community service projects

Prayer Meeting

Tuesdays at 6:30 PM

Come together with fellow believers for a time of prayer, reflection, and spiritual support. We pray for our community, our world, and each other's needs.

  • Intimate gathering
  • Prayer requests welcome
  • Guided meditation
  • Spiritual support

Children's Ministry

Sundays at 10:00 AM

Age-appropriate programs for children during our main worship service. Kids learn about faith through stories, songs, crafts, and interactive activities.

  • Ages 3-12
  • Trained volunteers
  • Safe environment
  • Fun learning activities

Community Outreach

Saturdays at 9:00 AM

Put faith into action through various community service projects. We serve at local food banks, organize clothing drives, and support families in need.

  • Monthly service projects
  • All ages welcome
  • Local partnerships
  • Make a difference
