Our Mission

At Grace Community Church, our mission is to create a welcoming space where people from all walks of life can come together to worship, learn, and grow in faith. We believe in the transformative power of God's love and strive to share that love with our community through acts of service, compassion, and fellowship.

Our Vision

We envision a community where every person feels valued, loved, and empowered to live out their faith in meaningful ways. Our goal is to be a beacon of hope and healing in our neighborhood, offering support, guidance, and spiritual nourishment to all who seek it.

Our Values

  • Faith: We believe in the power of faith to transform lives and communities.
  • Love: We practice unconditional love and acceptance for all people.
  • Service: We are committed to serving our community and those in need.
  • Fellowship: We foster meaningful relationships and community connections.
  • Growth: We encourage spiritual and personal growth for all members.

Our History

Grace Community Church was founded in 1994 by a small group of families who felt called to create a welcoming spiritual home in our community. What started as weekly gatherings in a local community center has grown into a thriving congregation of over 300 members.

Over the years, we have been blessed to build our current sanctuary, establish numerous community programs, and touch countless lives through our ministry. Our church has weathered challenges and celebrated victories, always maintaining our commitment to faith, love, and service.

Today, we continue to grow and evolve while staying true to our founding principles of inclusivity, compassion, and spiritual growth.
